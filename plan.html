CP1406/CP5638 - Assignment 1 - Project Plan

Name: ZHUO QIXUAN

Goal

Develop a professional website for 100SMILES Dental Care to showcase its dental services, team expertise, promotional offers, and oral health education. The site aims to increase new patient inquiries by 20% within 6 months, simplify access to clinic information for Razole residents, and enhance community awareness of preventive dental care.

Success Evaluation

1. Track new patient bookings that reference the website (via phone or email) and achieve a 20% increase compared to pre-launch data over 6 months.
2. Achieve a redemption rate of at least 10% for the "20% off first visit" promotion advertised on the site.
3. Receive positive feedback from 70%+ of users surveyed, indicating the website helped them make informed decisions about dental care.
4. Ensure the website functions smoothly across major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) with no broken links or layout issues.

Target Audience

Primary audience includes: Families in Razole (25-45 years old) who make healthcare decisions for children (5-12) and elderly relatives; individuals seeking preventive care (check-ups, cleanings); patients needing orthodontic treatments (braces, Invisalign); and emergency cases requiring same-day appointments. They value clear information, professional credentials, and convenient access to services via mobile and desktop devices.

Site Flowchart

WireFrames

1. Home Page: Navigation bar (Home, Services & Team, Dental Tips, Contact); hero section with clinic introduction; promotions banner; 3-column service highlights (preventive care, orthodontics, emergency services); and operating hours.
2. Services & Team Page: Left column lists service categories with brief descriptions; right column features team profiles with professional photos and qualifications.
3. Dental Tips Page: 4 educational articles with relevant images, formatted with short paragraphs and bullet points for readability.
4. Contact Page: Contact details (phone, email, address); operating hours; and location map.
